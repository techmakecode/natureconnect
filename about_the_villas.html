ABOUT US

It is our endeavor at Nature Connect to create spaces that render an experience of luxury living blended with nature for our patrons. We are driven by a common passion that drives lives from across the globe to Goa, as one of the front runners amongst destinations to live a quaint yet Modern life .

Our understanding and knowledge of the locations is deep-rooted and gives us an edge to select the right spots that have an abundance of natural beauty and sereneness. Our residences are carefully crafted to blend natural living with contemporary luxury lifestyle.

Come with us on this journey of finding self again, as we create spaces that effortlessly embody life as it should be. All our villas are managed by our partner Calithea Hospitality LLP. under their brand name VILLAASA which is a platinum standard in Hospitality management.
