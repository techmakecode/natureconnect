A MORNING WALK IN SALVADOR DO MUNDO

Salvador do Mundo is a little village in Bardez taluka blessed with nature’s play of soft and intense blue-greens round the year. The village is situated alongside river Mapusa with views of paddy fields, hillocks, creek expanse, rainforest, Salim Ali bird Sanctuary and rare migratory birds outlining the horizon.
